Data Integrity Notes
How conversion is counted
Converted = referral status is "Engaged In Program" - intake completed
Pending
Status is "Prospect" or "Prospect Intake Scheduled" - appointment may still happen. Not a failed referral.
Did not convert
Status is "Did Not Engage In Program" - includes: unable to contact, client declined, ineligible, duplicate referral, or referred to another program. These are closed and excluded from the pending count.
Denominator
Conversion rate = Engaged In Program ÷ all referrals received (excluding duplicates). Pending referrals are shown separately - not included in the rate.
