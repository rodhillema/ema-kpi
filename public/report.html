What counts
Any track with a closed status recorded during the period, regardless of when the track started.
Excluded
Did Not Initiate Sessions (moms who enrolled but never attended a first session). They surface separately in the intake-to-track funnel; a mom can't fail to complete a track she never started. Same-day close/reopen duplicates are also excluded (data-entry pattern used to change track type, not a real exit).
Known limitation
EP and RR launched recently, so moms who enrolled late had less time to finish. Lower completion rates for those tracks are expected at this stage, not a concern.
