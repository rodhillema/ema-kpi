What counts
Knowledge change has two views. Domains Improved counts each domain where the post score is higher than the pre score – direction of growth. Overall Change sums every item across all domains and shows the percent difference from pre to post – magnitude of growth. Scale is 1 to 5 (Strongly Disagree → Strongly Agree).
Minimum completion
ĒMA requires 75% curriculum completion. NPP: 8 of 10 lessons. EP: 6 of 8 lessons. RR: 3 of 4 lessons. The pill below shows whether this mom meets the completion standard.
Excluded
Domains with no change or a lower score are not counted as improved. Moms missing either a pre or post assessment are not measured.
Known limitation
In Overall Change, all items count equally. Domains with more items pull the overall number more strongly than smaller ones.
