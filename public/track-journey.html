Curriculum Detail i
How a lesson counts
Each distinct lesson in the curriculum counts once. A lesson is complete when the mom actually participated — determined by delivery type:
1:1 Sessions
Session marked Held + mom’s attendance is On time or Late. “No show” does not count even if the session is recorded as Held.
Group Sessions
Session marked Held + mom’s attendance is Present. Absent does not count. No attendance record (e.g. imported sessions) counts as attended.
Track Thresholds
Nurturing Parenting Program · 10 lessons
Empowered Parenting · 8 lessons
Roadmap to Resilience · 4 lessons
Excluded
Support sessions, planned sessions, and sessions with no lesson template attached (shown as “Additional Track Sessions” below the curriculum).
Assessment Detail i
EP & RR
Source: AssessmentResult + per-question responses from the matching template (Empowered Parenting Pre/Post or Resilience Pre/Post-Assessment). Bars show each question’s score on a 1–5 scale; the overall composite averages per-question responses.
NPP
Source: AAPIScore — 5 constructs (A–E) with paired pre/post values on a 1–10 sten scale. Used only when no EP/RR AssessmentResult exists for the pairing.
Excluded
PromiseServes (Legacy) assessments are pre-Trellis migration data and never count.
